My name is Destin Nguyen and I am currently a first year student at the University of California, Riverside. I am majoring in Psychology because of my passion for trying to understand people. I am 19 years old and I was born and raised in San Jose, California. I am fluent in both English and Vietnamese. I am currently seeking a job opportunity that will be able to help me grow as a person and give me the proper experience I will need for the future. I am a very hard working individual that aims for nothing but perfection. I believe in punctuality and efficiency. The two rules I grew up with were: if you are early, you are on time, but if you are on time, you are late, and work smarter not harder. These two phrases have been instilled in me from when I was very young and it has stuck with me since.
